## Notion - Global Config
- I use **Notion** as a mini "workflow". In general, I use this app for organizate all my code tasks, tasks to do, tasks in process, and tasks complete.
- This app is very nice to organizate your work, and now it's true better because it have the Notion AI, you can ask to the Notion AI to create for you custom apps for your workflow and tasks library.
- I recommend this app for all developers are reading this in this moment.
